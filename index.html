Welcome to Project Intraweb.

At Project Intraweb, we want everyone to have access to information, no matter if they are online or offline. Here are some apps that are installed that can work offline.

Nextcloud

An open source and private to store documents over the cloud.

RocketChat

An open sourced and also awesome alternative to Slack. You can communicate to anyone in the Intraweb using RocketChat.

Zimply

Search and read Wikipedia and other forms of information offline. This version includes everything but pictures.

GitLab

Our offline version of Github. Create and clone repositories offline with Gitlab.

Phet

Offline simulations provided by Phet. NOTE: Not all simulations are provided. Only HTML5 simualtions are provided at this moment.

Webmin

Console for admin. Access is resticted to admins only. Monitor the Pi's resources with a dashbaord, plus control tasks and other stuff right from Webmin.

Plex

Download, watch, and share videos that you either own, or created. Plus, with internet connection, you can even watch movies for free.
